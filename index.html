About App

Online Pharmacy App

The Online Pharmacy App by Charbindu offers a seamless way for users to order medications from their nearest pharmacies. With a simple and intuitive interface, customers can easily browse available products, view nearby pharmacies, and place orders for home delivery or pickup. The app allows users to send prescription details directly to pharmacies and track the status of their orders in real-time.
